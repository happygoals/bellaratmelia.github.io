Haemin Ryu

Haemin is an independent researcher and a software developer interested in Accessibility, Inclusive Design, Usability Studies, and Virtual Reality.

Her current research focuses on Menu interfaces to reduce the risky disembodiment situation in Virtual Reality.

After she received Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) degree at Purdue University Fort Wayne, she joined CirrusABS Corporation (Marketsnare) as a software developer for creating modern, engaging full-stack user experiences in web development.

In her free time, she enjoys hiking to enjoy nature and refresh herself. She loves cooking and playing the bass guitar and piano.
