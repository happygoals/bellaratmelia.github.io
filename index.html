Haemin Ryu

Fort Wayne, IN, USA

Haemin is a software developer and a researcher passionate about Inclusive Design, Accessibility, and Virtual Reality.

Her current research focuses on Menu interfaces to reduce the risky disembodiment situation in Virtual Reality.

After she received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Purdue University Fort Wayne, she joined CirrusABS Corporation (Marketsnare) as a software developer for creating full-stack user experiences from accessible and friendly, to conceptual and high-end projects in the multidisciplinary web development.

In her free time, she goes hiking to enjoy nature and refresh herself. She loves cooking and playing the bass guitar and piano.
