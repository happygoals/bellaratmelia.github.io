Haemin Ryu

Fort Wayne, IN, USA

Haemin is a researcher and a software developer interested in Accessibility, Inclusive Design, Usability Studies, and Virtual Reality.

Her current research focuses on Menu interfaces to reduce the risky disembodiment situation in Virtual Reality.

After she received a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science (BSCS) from Purdue University Fort Wayne, she joined CirrusABS Corporation (Marketsnare) as a software developer for creating full-stack user experiences from accessible and friendly, to conceptual and high-end projects in the multi-disciplinary web development.

In her free time, she goes hiking to enjoy nature and refresh herself. She loves cooking and playing the bass guitar and piano.
