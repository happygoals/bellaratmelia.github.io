PLUR

: Play Learn Unity Respect


This project was submitted to the Hack for Humanity: Hackathon for Social Goods for the Finals.

PLUR is a web app that uses local resources and crowdsourcing to build a strong database of programs and facilities that are accessible for individuals with disabilities,while raising awareness about anti-bullying and inclusion in sports. It is an online platform for physical education community for individuals with disabilities.

How might we bring equal sports education opportunity to impaired individuals and communities and share together in a moment of shared humanity?

Background

Sports can bring individuals and communities together in a moment of shared humanity. This vastly profitable industry also can cause damage to the environment, exploit the vulnerable, and be politicized to do harm. How can technology dismantle the inequity that occurs in sports?


Problem Statement

- Almost any sport can be modified for participation by individuals with a disability. Yet persons with disabilities are not encouraged, sometimes even discouraged to try a sport.

- Even though there are sports organizations that help towards making sports be more accessible, their impact is limited to a city or region. Outside of school the proportion of young people with a disability taking part in sports in last year was just 56% compared to 87% of young people in the general population.


Solution

A sports community platform for impaired people can connect with the professional coaches who can guide and assist them towards the sport of their interests.

My Role

- Created prototyping for web app that uses local resources and crowdsourcing to make sports programs and facilities more accessible to individuals with disabilities.

- Developed scenarios for connecting differently abled children and young adults with verified local coaches, trainers, sports associations, and clubs to begin their journey in sports.

Prototype examples

Features

Connecting users to coaches

Connecting users with mentors, coaches and organizations to help them get started with sports.


Events Board

Where organizations and schools can promote sports events open for impaired people. They can request the event, invite people, orginize the event, or attend the event.


Community

Providing a platform that encourages open discussions on accessibility in inclusive sports. Also it highlights the inspiring stories of those who have tackled all odds and pursued their dream sport.




Security

Volunteers will be affiliated with an organization to ensure security.


Coaches will know their students disabilities beforehand.


Business Model

Impact


The product has the potential to impact the entire population of differently abled persons


There are more than 1 billion persons in the world have some form of disability.



It also impacts the way sports organizations and governments help persons with disability


Partner organizations and governments can easily mentor students and adults on how to make sports more accessible, how to be more involved and even encourage them to pursue sports at a professional level through events and meaningful connections.
